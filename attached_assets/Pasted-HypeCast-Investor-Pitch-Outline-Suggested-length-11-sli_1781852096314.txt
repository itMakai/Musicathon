Slide 2 — The Problem

Slide 3 — The Solution
Headline: "Enter a city and an artist. Get a personal hype podcast."
A ~5-minute audio episode built on demand, covering:
Show details (venue, date)
Predicted setlist & song themes
Chorus cues and trivia to learn before the show
A real instrumental music bed under a radio-DJ narration
Three vibe modes: Radio DJ, Cinematic, Bestie.
